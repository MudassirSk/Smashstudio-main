Our Story

Bringing the Streets of India to Southall

At Chaat House, we believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's an experience. We use generations-old recipes, blending rich, aromatic spices to recreate the vibrant, bustling atmosphere of Indian street food vendors.

Whether you are craving the tangy crunch of our signature Samosa Chaat or the deep, comforting warmth of a slow-cooked Biryani, we guarantee a culinary journey you won't forget.
